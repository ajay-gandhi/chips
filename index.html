Chips

Use your computer while eating chips

  • Download for Mac
  • View On GitHub

This project is maintained by ajay-gandhi and mrkev

Chips is interactive

Just say your commands and Chips listens. Send texts (even through Facebook), open apps, Google things - the possibilities are probably endless.

Chips is extendible

We expose a really straightforward interface for writing your own modules. If you can think it, you can do it! To get started, take a look at the included modules in the modules directory.

Chips is usable

Chips lives in your menu bar - not as an annoying app that's always open. If you want to see Chips work, you can always open the window. If not, let it do its thing in the background. Chips will notify you when it completes your commands, or if it can't.

Get Chips!

Download Chips for Mac or look at the source code using the links on the left. We don't support Windows, but you should definitely be able to run Chips on Windows with just a few small tweaks.

Chips is interactive

Just say your commands and Chips listens. Send texts (even through Facebook), open apps, Google things - the possibilities are probably endless.

Chips is extendible

We expose a really straightforward interface for writing your own modules. If you can think it, you can do it!

Chips is usable

Chips lives in your menu bar - not as an annoying app that's always open. If you want to see Chips work, you can always open the window. If not, let it do its thing in the background. Chips will notify you when it completes your commands, or if it can't.
